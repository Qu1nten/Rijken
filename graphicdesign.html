Graphic Design

I started getting into graphic design around December of 2019. I have experimented with logo design, creating my own logos and small emblems. My work has also taken me into more artbased projects.

I am proficient at Sketchup and have decent experience with Adobe illustrator. I also know my way around FireAlpaca.

Adobe Illustator

I started learning Adobe Illustrator in late December of 2019. Illustrator is used for graphic (logo) design. The image on the left is my first attempt at a graphic that used the Golden ratio. This is a set of geometric shapes that gives a pleasing shape. I started by making the ratio shown on the left side of the image. I then created a shape resembling a bird using the circles created inside of the ratio. When I was happy with the shape I created a colour palatte with a few gradients that complimented the shape.
