ArchViz

Archviz, short for architectural visualization, is the process of creating realistic digital representations of architectural designs, allowing clients to visualize and assess the aesthetics and functionality of a building before it is constructed. my experience includes utilizing industry-standard software such as Lumion, D5 Renderer and Sketchup to create immersive and high-quality architectural visualizations that bring designs to life with realistic lighting and textures.
