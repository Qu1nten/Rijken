Reflection of the past

The building consists of four facades on which large mirrors are placed. These reflect the landscape, giving it the same appearance as the surroundings. The building has a concrete structure that is finished in a round shape on the inside. It can be seen as a sculpture or artwork and is intended to be used recreationally. The ideal location for this building is far removed from human infrastructure, embracing its massive size in the vast landscape.

I have tried to incorporate lyrics of the song ‘Goodbye to a world’ by Porter Robinson, into the design of the building. The mirrors are a reference to the lyric"give life to a world," which is achieved by the mirrors reflecting the landscape and environment. I have also taken the phrase "I will surround you" literally by creating the arches that surround you when you stand in the middle of the building.
