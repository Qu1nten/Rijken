Minecraft

Minecraft is a game that I have been playing since 2010. In this game you are free to create anything, from a small house to a big city. For around 3 years now I have been building up a portfolio of the projects I have created in the game. I currently work as a freelancer creating work for the marketplace run by Mojang. Along with paid commision work this has allowed me to work part time.

My experience

I started doing professional work a few years ago starting with small commisions at a team called Synthria. After having worked for the team for about a year I joined another team called Meraki. Meraki was a high quality build team doing bigger commision work. I stayed with Meraki for around 2 years.

Shortly after leaving I joined EntityBuilds, they were a Minecraft marketplace based team. After some time working for EntityBuilds I decided to join Sapphire Studios, this is the team I have currently been working for around 1 year. They were previously a commision based team but are now fully focused on Marketplace.

Albanese

Albanese was my first marketplace project released in 2018 as a trial for a team called EntityBuilds. It is based on a mix of italian and mexican architecture. I built it up by creating and coloring the terrain. After that I created the roads and placed down the location for the structures. At the end I added details, interior and nature around like trees and bushes.

Personal projects

Not every build I create is a commision or a project for the Marketplace. Most builds were personal, solo, projects done for either my portfolio, school or to have fun making something in my free time.

Contests

I have also taken part in a few build contests, 2 of them hosted by PlanetMinecraft and a few done on BuildersRefuges I took part in these contests to learn more skills and become faster at building as for some of the contests there was a short time limit.
