Minecraft

Minecraft is a game that I have been playing since 2010. In this game you are free to create anything, from a small house to a big city. For around 4 years now I have been building up a portfolio of the projects I have created in the game. I currently work as a freelance artist creating work for the Bedrock Marketplace run by Mojang. Along with paid commision work this has allowed me to work part time creating what I love.

All images shown below are solo creations.

My experience

One of the biggest Bedrock servers out there, I am currently creating Marketplace, bedrock and java content as a designer for Cubecraft games.
