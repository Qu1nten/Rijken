Minecraft

Minecraft is a game that I have been playing since 2010. In this game you are free to create anything, from a small house to a big city. For around 3 years now I have been building up a portfolio of the projects I have created in the game. I currently work as a freelance artist creating work for the marketplace run by Mojang. Along with paid commision work this has allowed me to work part time.

My experience

I started doing professional work a few years ago starting with small commisions at a team called Synthria. After having worked for the team for about a year I joined another team called Meraki. Meraki was a high quality build team doing bigger commision work. I stayed with Meraki for around 2 years.

Shortly after leaving Meraki I joined EntityBuilds, I was able to create my first Marketplace map together with EntityBuilds. Though sadly after my first map I had to take a break from building to focus on college. In 2018 I joined Sapphire Studios, I was able to do commision and Marketplace work at Sapphire Studios for 1 1/2 years until, at the start of 2020 I joined Shaliquinn's Schematics. I currently do work for Shaliquinn's Schematics making Marketplace content.

Black Forest Keep

A map inspired by medieval architecture and fantasy castles, this map was my first project for Shaliquinn's Schematics

Original Luckyblocks

Original Luckyblocks is the largest solo project I have been able to create working for Sapphire Studios. The build takes inspiration from Italian structure and focuses around the mansion looking over the village on the hillside. The composition of the build takes inspiration from my first marketplace map 'Albanese'.

Albanese

Albanese was my first marketplace project released in 2018 as a trial for a team called EntityBuilds. It is based on a mix of Italian and Mexican architecture. I built it up by creating and coloring the terrain. After that I created the roads and placed down the location for the structures. At the end I added details, interior and nature around like trees and bushes.

Personal projects

Not every build I create is a commision or a project for the Marketplace. My main reasons for creating personal projects is for self-improvement, building up a portfolio, contests but most importantly enjoyment
