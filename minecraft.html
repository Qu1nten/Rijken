Minecraft

Minecraft is a game that I have been playing since 2010. In this game you are free to create anything, from a small house to a big city. For around 4 years now I have been building up a portfolio of the projects I have created in the game. I currently work as a freelance artist creating work for the Bedrock Marketplace run by Mojang. Along with paid commission work this has allowed me to work part time creating what I love.

All images shown below are solo creations.

My experience

A Marketplace team where I work on smaller scale projects and do occasional render work.

One of the biggest Bedrock servers out there, I have created Marketplace, bedrock and java content as a designer for Cubecraft games. And have been able to help build on one of their biggest RPGs 'Magic and Mayhem' by constructing Alunore, Yhornheim and the image shown on the left, Quintenia.

Shaliquinn's Schematics / December 2019 - November 2020
